THE PROBLEM STATEMENT

My game aims to transform the educational experience for children with learning disabilities by providing a more inclusive and accessible approach to learning. Many children struggle in traditional education systems because the curriculum often caters to a narrow range of learning styles, leaving those with unique needs at a disadvantage. This lack of support can hinder their ability to thrive and reach their full potential. My goal is to create a series of engaging learning games designed specifically to accommodate diverse learning styles, empowering children with learning disabilities to succeed from an early age in a way that truly meets their needs.

MECHANICS

The interactive multi-level experience is designed to help children develop essential educational skills in a fun and engaging way. Each level introduces unique challenges and games, carefully crafted to meet the individual needs of every child. As they progress, they’ll build key skills like problem-solving, memory, and attention to detail, all while enjoying the learning process. With 4 levels to explore, each featuring its own set of exciting activities, children can earn colourful stickers as they complete each stage. This rewarding journey keeps them motivated and excited to keep learning, with every level offering a new opportunity for growth and achievement!

THE FACTORS BOOK

STORY

Step into the world of Lilly the Little Farmer, where every adventure brings an opportunity to learn and grow. As Lilly rescues her furry friends, she faces challenges that only you can help her overcome. Through fun and interactive educational games, players develop essential skills while advancing through the story. Each game adapts to different difficulty levels, ensuring a rewarding experience for every little learner.

THE FACTORS PROGRESS APP

Introducing the Factors app – designed to make learning progress effortless for both parents and teachers. With this app, users can easily connect, delegate tasks, and communicate in real time, ensuring that children receive the maximum support and guidance needed for their educational journey. Whether you're a parent looking to stay updated on your child's development or a teacher coordinating with families, the Factors app streamlines the process, creating a collaborative learning environment that fosters success.
